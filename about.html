About Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to think and act like humans. Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. Together, AI and ML are revolutionizing industries, enabling advancements in areas such as healthcare, finance, and autonomous systems. Our department focuses on equipping students with the skills to excel in this rapidly evolving field.
